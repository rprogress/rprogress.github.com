Other Ways to Give

Your contribution makes it possible for Redefining Progress to continue its work at the local, state, and national level. There are many ways to give.


Monthly Contribution

By making a commitment to support Redefining Progress on a monthly basis, you make it possible for us to better plan for sustainability. As a Sustaining Contributor—unless you direct otherwise—you will receive updates on critical developments in your area of interest. Set up your automatic monthly payments using our secure server.

Donate Stocks and Securities

Appreciated assets are a powerful tool that gives you a vehicle to invest in Redefining Progress while helping us build internal capacity to address a widening scope of issues in communities across the country. Please contact Y. Armando Nieto at 510.444.3041, ext. 316 to discuss your donation.

Memorial and Honorary Donations

Consider making a donation in memory of a loved one or honored friend as a way of expressing your commitment to the future of our planet. Gifts can be made online using our secure server, via mail using our donation form, or by calling 510.444.3041, ext. 318.

Bequests and Planned Gifts

Perhaps the most personal statement you can make, a planned gift or bequest to Redefining Progress as part of your estate planning ensures that we continue to provide a platform for innovative thinking and action for generations to come. Please contact Y. Armando Nieto at 510.444.3041, ext. 316 to discuss your gift.

Please contact Y. Armando Nieto, Development Director, at 510.444.3041, ext 316, to discuss these and other opportunities for giving.
