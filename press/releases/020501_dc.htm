FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE:
DATE: May 1, 2002
CONTACT:
Craig Cheslog (Redefining Progress: 510-444-3041)

BUSINESS LEADERS WILL DISCUSS CLIMATE CHANGE POLICY AT
MAY 8TH CAPITOL HILL BRIEFING

OAKLAND, Calif.—Environmental Entrepreneurs (E2) and Redefining Progress will sponsor a Capitol Hill briefing to explore how entrepreneurship must be a key component of any solution to the climate change problem. The briefing will also explore how a market-based climate strategy would provide growth opportunities for some companies, be neutral for many, and generate revenue that could aid the industries, workers, and publics most affected by the transition.

The briefing, Climate Change Policies and Business Opportunities, will be held on Wednesday, May 8, from 9:30 a.m.-12:00 noon in the Dirksen Senate Office Building, Room SD-406 (The Committee on Environment and Public Works Hearing Room).

WHAT: Climate Change Policies and Business Opportunities briefing including two panels: 1) Climate Change: Entrepreneurial Challenges and Solutions, and 2) Why Taking Action on Climate is Good for the Economy.

WHO: The following speakers will participate at the briefing: Robert Epstein, Co-Founder, Sybase and Founder, Environmental Entrepreneurs (E2); Michel Gelobter, Executive Director, Redefining Progress; Frank Dixon, Managing Director, Innovest Strategic Value Advisors; David Hawkins, Climate Center Program Director, Natural Resources Defense Council; Frank Mazanec, Senior Vice President, OnSight Energy; Diana Propper, Co-Founder and Managing Director, EA Capital; and Alexandra Deane Thornton, Executive Director, Center for a Sustainable Economy.

WHEN: May 8, 2002 from 9:30 a.m.-12 noon.

WHERE: Dirksen Senate Office Building, Room SD-406 (The Committee on Environment and Public Works Hearing Room)

Redefining Progress is a nonpartisan public policy organization based in Oakland, California that creates policies and tools to encourage accurate market prices, protect our common social and natural assets, and foster social and economic sustainability.

Environmental Entrepreneurs (E2) is a national community of professionals and business people who believe in protecting the environment while building economic prosperity.
