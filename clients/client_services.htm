Client Services

Our contract research and analysis services enable policymakers, advocates, and other concerned individuals and groups to select policies that provide broad public benefits without unintended side-effects.


Indicators Analysis

Redefining Progress offers Indicators Analysis services to businesses, community groups, governments, and schools. Our Indicators Analysis is based on two well-respected and peer-reviewed scientific tools: the Ecological Footprint and the Genuine Progress Indicator. We perform the following analyses on a fee-for-service basis:

  • Municipal Footprint Analysis
  • Business Footprint Analysis
  • Genuine Progress Indicator Analysis
  • Scenarios for Sustainability Analysis

Economic Analysis

We also do Economic Analysis of policies at the local, state and federal level, with special expertise in climate and energy policy, market incentives for sustainability, macroeconomic analysis, and distributional analysis. Our expertise includes:
  • Public policy analysis
  • Market-based approaches to environmental problems
  • Macroeconomic analysis
  • Distributional and social justice analysis

For more information about our client services, please contact John Talberth (Indicators Analysis) at 510.444.3041, ext. 317; or James Barrett (Economic Analysis) at 202. 234.9665. Or, request a quote.
