Research and Publications

Redefining Progress measures the impact of policies and practices on people, communities, and the environment. Our research is innovative and objective, and is based on replicable models and scientific frameworks.

Our publications encompass in-depth economic analysis; updates of, improvements to, and adaptation of the Ecological Footprint and the Genuine Progress Indicator; and research into the effects of climate change on vulnerable populations.

Our publications, with few exceptions, are provided for free. Please donate what you think these resources are worth.

Available for purchase are The Community Indicators Handbook, 2nd Edition; Tax Waste, Not Work; and the Ecological Footprint Quiz CD, among others. For bulk orders, please contact communications@rprogress.org.
